Privacy Policy

Information Collection

ACTMS collects information to provide transparent and secure tender management services. We collect data through your interactions with our platform, including account creation, bid submissions, and system usage.

Types of information collected include company details, contact information, bid proposals, and platform usage analytics for security and improvement purposes.

Data Usage

Your data is used to facilitate tender processes, prevent fraud through AI analysis, and improve our services. We employ advanced security measures to protect your information.

  • Tender and bid processing
  • Fraud detection and prevention
  • Platform security and monitoring
  • Service improvement and analytics

Data Protection

ACTMS implements industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure data transmission, and regular security audits to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or concerns, contact us at privacy@actms.gov or through our official contact channels.

Getting Started

System Overview

ACTMS is a comprehensive platform designed to ensure transparency and prevent corruption in government tender processes through AI-powered fraud detection.

Key Features

  • Transparent tender management
  • Secure bid submission process
  • Real-time fraud detection
  • Comprehensive analytics dashboard
  • AI-powered insights and recommendations

Tender Management

Creating Tenders

Navigate to the Tender Management page to create new tenders:

  1. Fill in tender details (title, department, budget, deadline)
  2. Provide comprehensive description and requirements
  3. Upload supporting documents
  4. Review and publish

Monitoring Tenders

Use the analytics dashboard to monitor tender performance, track bid submissions, and analyze department-wise distribution.

Bid Submission

Multi-Step Process

The bid submission follows a secure 5-step process:

  1. Select Tender: Choose from active tenders
  2. Company Info: Provide company details and registration
  3. Bid Details: Enter bid amount and proposal
  4. Upload Documents: Attach required documentation
  5. Review & Submit: Verify and digitally sign

AI Features

Fraud Detection

Our AI system analyzes multiple factors to detect potential fraud:

  • Bid amount anomalies and pricing patterns
  • Document similarity analysis
  • Company network connections
  • Submission timing patterns
  • Historical behavior analysis

Model Training

The system continuously learns and improves through regular model training using new data and feedback from fraud investigations.
